Anatomy of the shell

Three regions: sidebar (project + nav, persistent), headerbar (breadcrumb + global actions, sticky), body (the screen). Cost & daemon health are always visible in the sidebar footer.

Density & theme

  • Dark theme only for v1. Light theme later if asked — variables are theme-token based.
  • Two density modes (toggle in Settings): Comfortable (default) and Compact. Compact reduces row padding ~30%.
  • Mono font for IDs, paths, statuses. Sans for prose. Diagrams and previews always mono.

Responsive plan

  • ≥1280px: full shell, two/three-column body where relevant.
  • 900–1280: sidebar collapses to 64px icon-only with hover-expand.
  • <900: sidebar becomes drawer (⌘\ to toggle). Body single-column.
  • Mobile: out of scope for v1; deep-links still work readably.

Empty states

No projects

Show "Create your first project" CTA + a 3-step wizard (name, GH repo, default base branch).

No daemons paired

Sidebar footer becomes a "Pair your laptop" call-to-action with the one-line command.

No plans yet

Plans tab shows "Start with a template" + import-from-markdown CTA.

No live sessions

Dashboard shows recent sessions (last 24h) instead of live ones, plus a "Spawn from a breakdown" hint.
